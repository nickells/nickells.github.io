About

Hello! I am Nick Ellsworth, and this is my online portfolio. Welcome. Take a look around. Stop and smell the DOM tree.

After completing my music major in Clarinet Performance, I enrolled in Fullstack Academy to continue nurturing my need to build and create. I spent three months in their intensive course, learning the ins and outs of MEAN stack Javascript. Afterwards, I was selected to join the Fullstack team for a Fellowship position, which is part teaching-assistant and part software engineer. I spent another 13 weeks there, gaining valuable experience mentoring others and working on an Agile team. In February 2016, I started doing web development at re#, a production and development consultancy based in Brooklyn.
